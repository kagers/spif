SPIF for Researchers

Planetary science researchers, whether Cornell faculty, graduate researchers, or undergraduate researchers, all have access to SPIF's extensive collection of planetary image data and database indexes that make finding the necessary data from the SPIF collection a simple task.

Researchers from as far away as Canada have used SPIF data in their research.

Upcoming plans to make the databases accessible online will make searching the SPIF collection even easier, and available to a wider audience.
