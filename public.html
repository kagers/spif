SPIF for the Public

As a member of the Regional Planetary Image Facility Network (RPIF-N), SPIF is open to the public.

Regular hours are Monday through Friday, 9am to 4:30pm. Special hours by appointment.

Please feel free to stop in if you are in the area. We are located on the 3rd floor of the Space Sciences Building, Cornell University, in Ithaca, NY.

For group visits, we request that you schedule visits in advance. Please call (607) 255-3833 or e-mail Rick KlineThis e-mail address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it , SPIF Data Manager, for scheduling.

Tour the Solar System at SPIF!

  • Touch a meteorite
  • See the flag and patch presented to Cornell that flew aboard the Space Shuttle Columbia
  • View photographs of the solar system
  • Watch a live broadcast from NASA (when available)
